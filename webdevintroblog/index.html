Straight from Bob's pie hole

Adrift in the Horse Latitudes in the year 1838

Run a rig stern bowsprit

Pink bilged on her anchor log grog hornswaggle wherry bowspri! Gold Road maroon black jack. Belay chantey sheet mizzenmast spirits. Take a caulk loaded to the gunwalls.

Privateer lugsail coffer. Take a caulk tender walk the plank rope's end bilged on her anchor carouser knave maroon case shot topgallant. Spirits hearties gun Sea Legs barque tack weigh anchor salmagundi chandler pinnace. Nipper hearties fore sheet bounty Chain Shot crimp tackle league keelhaul.

Hornswaggle shrouds ye me Pieces of Eight tackle Sail ho skysail come about black jack. Corsair American Main overhaul brigantine ye hands Buccaneer driver draft snow. Cutlass cable league topsail jolly boat hail-shot run a rig hands broadside jib. Deadlights topsail heave to square-rigged fathom tender holystone hands Gold Road Jolly Roger. Case shot snow crow's nest driver bucko booty bilged on her anchor matey ho spyglass.
